Cold Brew

"What is Cold Brew, the beloved coffee of hot summer days, and how is Cold Brew brewed and made?"

What is the difference
between Cold Brew coffee
and "regular" coffee?

Cold Brew is a smoother-tasting coffee
thanks to its slow brewing and preparation method.

How is
Cold Brew
made?

Put 100-150 grams of coarsely ground coffee
on top of 1 liter of cold water and let it steep for 12-16 hours.
Afterwards, strain the coffee concentrate through a filter.
That's it!
Mix it with ice or other beverages according to your taste.
It's entirely up to your palate.
